About

BookSafe is an app that aims to help you keep track of all your accounts in the most convenient, non-invasive way possible.

Does BookSafe have any of my data?

No, BookSafe stores none of your data. Your data is only saved on your device.

Is BookSafe supported on mobile devices?

Yes, as BookSafe is supported on all devices.
